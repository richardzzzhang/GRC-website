Our Story

Building a Legacy
One Project at a Time

GRC Brown is part of the Global Research and Consulting Group — an internationally-oriented 501(c)(3) nonprofit with chapters at universities across the world. Our mission is to drive global social impact and empower students to become responsible, purpose-driven leaders.

Since launching, we've delivered pro-bono consulting to organizations including Red Cross, Goodwill, Make-A-Wish, and Red Pencil. Our clients receive the same quality of strategic support as a professional firm — at no cost.
