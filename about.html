Our Story

Building a Legacy
Since 2020

The Global Research and Consulting (GRC) Group is an internationally-oriented educational non-profit with an expansive network of students from universities all over the world. Our mission is to drive global social impact change and empower students to become responsible future leaders.

We have completed over 60 projects with past clients including UNESCO, World Bank, Wikipedia, Oxfam, WWF, and many more.

Founded in Fall 2020, GRC Brown has already established itself as a successful, innovative student-run consulting organization and think tank on campus, delivering advisory and analytics solutions that exceed client expectations.
