Our Mission

Driving Meaningful
Global Change

GRC Brown is a chapter of GRC — a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to helping global NGOs and social impact startups achieve their goals while empowering students to give back to the global community.

We pair Brown's most passionate students with nonprofits tackling real-world challenges — delivering pro-bono strategic consulting at no cost to our clients.
