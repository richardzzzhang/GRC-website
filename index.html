Our Mission

Driving Meaningful
Global Change

Our mission is to help socially-oriented organizations bring meaningful global change. Our consultants are top students from Brown University, trained specifically to understand how to tackle nonprofits' and social impact organizations' biggest challenges.

Founded in Fall 2020, GRC Brown has established itself as a successful, innovative student-run consulting organization and think tank, offering advisory and analytics solutions across business development, revenue enhancement, and new market expansion.
