FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a client?

Reach out via email or fill out the contact form. We'll schedule an initial consultation to understand your needs and how we can help.

Is consulting really free?

Yes — all of our consulting engagements are completely pro-bono. We believe mission-driven organizations shouldn't be priced out of high-quality strategic support.

What types of organizations do you work with?

We work primarily with NGOs, nonprofits, and social impact organizations. Past clients include Red Cross, Goodwill, Make-A-Wish, and Red Pencil.

How can I join GRC Brown?

We recruit new members each semester at Brown. If you're a student passionate about consulting and social impact, reach out to learn about our application process.

What is a typical project timeline?

Most engagements run 8–12 weeks, aligned with our semester schedule. We work with you to establish a timeline that fits your needs.
