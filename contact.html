FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a client?

Reach out to us via email at grcbrown@brown.edu or fill out the contact form. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your needs and how we can help.

What types of organizations do you work with?

We primarily work with NGOs, nonprofits, and social impact startups across various sectors including international development, environmental conservation, education, healthcare, and more.

How can I join GRC Brown?

We recruit new members every September. If you're a Brown student interested in consulting and social impact, reach out to brown@grcglobalgroup.com to learn about our application process.

What is the typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity, but most engagements last between 8-12 weeks. We work with you to establish a timeline that meets your needs.
